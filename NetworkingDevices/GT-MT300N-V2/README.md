## About GT-MT300N-V2
- Mango (GL-MT300N-V2) has been upgraded with the new MT7628NN chip, doubling the RAM from 64MB to 128MB for smoother performance. Improved Wi-Fi stability from the MTK driver and faster OpenVPN encryption keep you securely connected. Whether you need reliable Wi-Fi while traveling or want to experiment with the 4 included GPIOs for DIY projects, the Mango is designed to meet your needs.
